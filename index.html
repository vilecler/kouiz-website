Features

Let me show you a little view of what you can do with Kouiz

Explore quizzes

Within the app you can explore a huge quantity of quizzes divided into community and official quizzes.

Explore themes

The official quizzes are sorted by categories and themes. Each theme keep your progression.

Create your own quizzes

If you are premium, you have the possibility to create your own quizzes within the app.

Play with your friends

Each quiz can be played in simultaneous whith several people you invited before, fiends for instance.

Messaging

An instant messaging system is included within the app supporting private messages and conversations.

Account Management

An account is required to access the app. It allows to keep your progression and manage all the features.

Quizzes

Let me present you some features regarding quizzes within Kouiz.

Official quizzes

In Kouiz, each user is giving access to a selection of quizzes made by the app. These quizzes are groupped by themes.

Community quizzes

It also exists quizzes which have been my by other players. These quizzes are called community quizzes.

Play with your friends

For every quiz in Kouiz, you have the option to play with friends in simultaneous.

Create your own quizzes

If you are premium, you have the opportunity to create your own community quizzes which can be played by everyone.

Rewards

For every quiz you play, you will be offered experience and also energy depending on the score you got.
